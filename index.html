About Us

Back in 1992, two brothers opened a sub shop and beverage center in Troy, New York called “Deli Express”. They sold a lot of beer and even more of their famous $.99 subs. Affordability and quality set them apart. A copyright dispute forced the brothers to rename the store the “Deli and Brew”.

In 1999, one of the Deli and Brew’s long-time beer salesmen, Ken Stulmaker took over the store. Since then, Ken has followed the ideals that made the Deli and Brew so popular to begin with.

Beer is an important part of the Deli and Brew but what really sets them apart are the gigantic subs and amazing pizza. Dough made daily, fresh rolls that are delivered 365 days a year, homemade sauce, double stuffed subs, mozzarella shredded 5-6 times a day, fresh vegetables sliced twice a day are all things you can expect from the Deli and Brew. Ken isn't concerned with profit, he’s obsessed with quality. That’s why your large sub has almost a pound of meat on it at an affordable price. You can feel good knowing that the Deli and Brew is going the extra mile to make sure you get the absolute best without emptying your wallet.

Prices are subject to change.
